A short presentation :

This project was made as part of the Emotional Game Jam 2025. Hasn’t been updated.

The two themes for this edition were: “IRREVERSIBLE” and “Fire Walk With Me.”
We decided to go with a dynamic 2D runner game with definitive choices made under pressure. The character is in a hurry and on fire, so he has to make quick decisions.
Our intention was to make a fun and entertaining game.

It was made on Unity.
The development of the game is done in C#.

The jury decided to nominate Morning Madness for the Game Jam's “coup de coeur ” award.
